Taj Madikeri Resort

Perched 1,220 m above sea level on a peaceful hillside is Taj Madikeri Resort & Spa Coorg. It features a large spa, which offers spectacular views of the rainforest from each treatment room, luxurious accommodations and 4 dining options. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Featuring panoramic views from every spacious room, they are equipped with a flat-screen TV, safe and mini-bar. For convenience, tea and coffee-making facilities are provided. The private bathroom comes with a bath or shower. 24-hour room service and free Wi-Fi is available. Guests can enjoy a relaxing swim at the outdoor swimming pool or maintain their fitness regiment at the fitness center. A variety of treatments are available at Jive Grande Spa, which spans 3 floors. Tours, nature walks and even cookery lessons can be arranged at the property. Ferntree offers an international menu at Ferntree, Taj Madikeri Resort & Spa Coorg's all-day dining restaurant, which features outdoor seating. Nellaki specializes in Coorg delicacies and Hive bar serves a range of alcoholic beverages. Dew is the on-site wellness cafe. Arrangements can be made for alternative dining experiences around the resort. This tranquil property is located 4.3 mi from Madikeri and 99 mi from the Mangalore Airport.
